About us

Our founder has always been found of bonsai plants. She wanted to share the beauty of these plants with the rest of the world which eventually lead to the creation of our store. Our store was founded in the year 2019 AD. We come from humble beginnings and currently are a small business consisting of 5 members. Our goal is to preserve this rare art form and captivate people. Neha Pokhrel
Hi, my name is Neha Pokhrel and I am the founder of this business. I am passionate about horticulture and the art of bonsai. I saw a need for high-quality bonsai plants and tools in the local community, and decided to open a store dedicated to providing these products. I believe that bonsai brings peace and tranquility to those who practice it and I wanted to share that experience with others. With a focus on customer satisfaction and expert advice, our store has become a hub for bonsai enthusiasts in our area.

Pritishma Kunwar
Hello, I am Pritishma Kunwar and I am the store manager of this business. I chose to work here because I have a passion for nature and horticulture. I find the art of bonsai to be fascinating and enjoy the challenge of helping customers select the perfect bonsai for their space. I also appreciate the calm and peaceful atmosphere that the store provides, which allows me to escape from the fast-paced world. I see this job as an opportunity to share my love of bonsai and bring a touch of nature into people's lives.
