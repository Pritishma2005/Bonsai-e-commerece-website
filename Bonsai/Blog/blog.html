How technology has affected daily life and busisness


Technology is a vital component of life in the modern world. Technology is important and useful in all areas of humman life including business today. It has made life comfortable by making commucation and transport faster and easier. We have become so addicted to this convienient lifestyle that imagining a world without technology seems unlivable.


However Technology has both positive and negative aspects.


Positive impacts

  1. Education is accessible easily.
  2. Smoother trade.
  3. Significantly improved the health sector.
  4. Promoting new business is much easier and cost-efficient.
  5. Easier communication and transportation.
  6. Amateur with no business expiriences are able learn and create their start-ups.
  7. Delivering products and getting feedback from the customers is much easier.
  8. People are able by and sell products with a single click.


Negative impacts

  1. It has decreased privacy.
  2. Cyber crimes have increased by a huge number.
  3. People has become prone to health risks such as eating disorders and anxiety.
  4. Face-toFace communication has decreased.
  5. High risk of business getting hacked.
  6. Some modern technology gadgets ans devices are costly and most small businesses cannot afford it.
  7. Employees and workers get distracted by modern technology during working time which decreases the effiiency of the business.
  8. Training new employees for the usage of modern technology gadgets for business is very difficult.
